About The Platform

This student productivity platform helps students organize their academic life. Users can track study hours, manage assignments and tasks, and stay motivated through a leaderboard ranking system. The platform encourages consistent study habits by rewarding productivity with points and showing progress statistics inside a personalized dashboard.

Main Features

Student Dashboard

After login students can view their total study hours, points earned, and productivity summary.

Task Management

Students can create tasks for homework, projects or exams and mark them as completed.

Study Hour Tracking

Students can log daily study hours to measure their learning progress.

Leaderboard

Students compete with each other based on weekly productivity points.
